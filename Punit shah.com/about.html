My Journey

From Humble Beginnings to Building Multi-Sector Ventures

From humble beginnings to building multi-sector ventures, my journey has been one of constant learning and evolution. I began my entrepreneurial path with City One Tourism in the UAE, crafting curated travel experiences that connected people across cultures. This foundation of service and authenticity became the blueprint for everything that followed.

City One Tourism

Curated travel experiences in the UAE, connecting people across cultures with authenticity and service excellence.

K Hotels

From construction to #25 and #14 rankings among Africa's top 25 hotels by TripAdvisor. One of Entebbe's most recognized properties.

Yuti Breweries

First mover in Uganda's mass-market beer and liquor production, inaugurated by H.E. the President in 2020, creating hundreds of jobs.

Emiboozi

A homegrown restaurant concept redefining the Ugandan dining scene with authentic flavors and modern hospitality.

Lumilife

Delivering quality lighting and automation solutions across apartments, mansions, hotels, and office blocks.

Rudra Agro

Trading cocoa and coffee while acquiring land for future cultivation. Impact-driven growth in agriculture.
